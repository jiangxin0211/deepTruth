What is the DeepTruth?

DeepTruth is an A.I. system developed to combat the distribution of misinformation online,addable to exiting browsers.

  • Analyzes online news and detects fake news
  • Filters out fake news from your social media news feeds(e.d.,Twitter,Facebook)
  • Filters out fake news from your news aggregator
